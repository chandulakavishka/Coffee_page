What is Vienna Mocha?

What is Vienna Mocha? Viennese coffee is a fancier type of coffee, very reminiscent of cafe mocha. The texture is very different, and this can set the two apart. It contains espresso, chocolate syrup, chocolate ganache, and a whole lot of whipped cream and toppings. Cinnamon, chocolate sprinkles, cocoa powder, whatever you like.

Vienna roast coffee is dark brown in color, with a bit of oil on the surface and a more bittersweet flavor than beans that roast for a more extended period of time.

Coffee that’s been infused with the flavor of coffee ice cream and rich mocha chunks. Fashioned after the legendary friendly’s ice cream; Vienna mocha chunk, this coffee can be enjoyed iced or hot anytime.

Or simply known as Vienna Coffee, this coffee is a fun-loving twist to your regular Espresso shot. What’s the twist, you ask? WHIPPED CREAM!

Ideal serving: 60ml Espresso + Whipped cream on the top in a 150ml cup.
